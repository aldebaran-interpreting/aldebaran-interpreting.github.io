How do I request a sign language interpreter? Please contact Aldebaran Interpreting Services at 315-955-5223, or email at thirdeyeterp@protonmail.com.

How far in advance should I request Interpreting Services? For best results, request should be made as soon as appointment or event is scheduled.

Why do I need to Provide an Interpreter? By using a Qualified Interpreter, you can be assured that the skills of the interpreter meet a national standard and satisfy ADA requirements, reducing your liability.

Can’t a friend or family member interpret? Communication is compromised when a friend or family member assumes the role of neutral, unbiased interpreter. Clients are entitled to a qualified interpreter.

Will my request need a team of interpreters? Due to the physical, mental and/ or logistical demands of the work some assignments, regardless of length, may require special preparation or teams. Professional judgment and experience will be used in making a final decision. Charges will apply per interpreter.

Who is responsible for paying the interpreter? Under Titles II&III of the ADA (Americans with Disabilities Actof 1990), the business entity, doctor’s office, dental practice, government agency, hospital, private organization, courthouse/jail, public entity, private or public school is responsible to provide communication access for individuals with disabilities. It is not the responsibility of the disabled individual to pay for accommodations.

How to interact in an interpreted environment

  • Speak clearly and at a normal pace.
  • Address each individual(s) personally and directly.
  • Be Patient. The interpreter may be a sentence or two behind the person communicating. This is part of the interpreting process. Expect slight delays in getting responses to questions or in the next “turn” in the conversation.
  • Allow the interpreter to place herself/himself appropriately.
  • Supply the interpreter with any materials, hand-outs, etc. for review, in order to facilitate a clearer interpretation. This is especially important with technical and detailed materials
  • Remember when making requests, we recommend doing so at least 2 weeks in advance to make sure we are able to confirm an interpreter.
  • Contact Aldebaran Interpreting Service with as much advance notice as possible. Requests may be made by telephone and email.
