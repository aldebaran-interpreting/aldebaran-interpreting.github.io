Aldebaran Interpreting and Third Eye Theater, was established by a freelance interpreter, certified Teacher of the Deaf and American Sign Language instructor. Aldebaran Interpreting is a small business, located in Jefferson County committed to providing quality professional interpreting services throughout the north country.

Aldebaran Interpreting is defined by dependability and excellence in community and performance interpreting. Diligence is given to helping your organization secure the service it needs. Aldebaran Interpreting has over two decades of experience in education, interpreting and consulting work. Respect for American Sign Language and the Deaf Community will translate into success for you.

Aldebaran Interpreting is dedicated to the proposition that no Deaf individual should be denied the ability to meet their full potential due to a lack of access to quality sign language or educational services. Confidential and smooth communication is a priority and helps assure that all consumers are satisfied.

ADA and Tax Incentives:

*The American's with Disabilities Act (ADA) Title III requires that an agency or facility providing services to the public must be accessible to consumers with disabilities. In the event that your agency/facility will be serving consumers who are deaf or hard of hearing, you may need to obtain the services of a sign language interpreter. Please visit our FAQ page for more information regarding the ADA, tax incentives, and how to work with an interpreter.
