Cancellation, No Shows

  • Cancellations on individual or intermittent requests, made less than two business days prior to the beginning of the assignment will be billed for the full amount. Cancellation policies on short term or long term contracts will be determined at the time of contract.
  • The full amount will be charged if the interpreter arrives to the assignment and either consumer or client does not. The interpreter will remain approximately 20 minutes before departing, after consultation with the organizer if possible. This will be applicable to all assignments with the full and agreed fee still payable.
  • No-shows are billed without exception.

No charges will be assessed if the interpreter must be absent from an assignment.

Please note, that NO SHOWS and LATE CANCELLATIONS are billed as scheduled.
