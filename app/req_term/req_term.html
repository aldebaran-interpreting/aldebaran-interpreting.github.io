Terms and Conditions

For a fee estimate please contact Aldebaran Interpreting Services at 315-955-5223 or at interpreting@aldebaraninterpretingservices.com

Most services require an initial 2 hour minimum fee. Additional hours are charged in quarter hour increments.

Travel Rate:
$.50/mile or hourly rate depending on situation.

Special Assignments:

  • Platform or similar assignments: Negotiated at time of request. Please see Third Eye Theater
  • Concerts and theatrical performances: Negotiated per assignment. Third Eye Theater Conferences: negotiated per assignment.
  • Due to the physical, mental and/ or logistical demands of the work some assignments, regardless of length, may require special preparation or teams. Professional judgment and experience will be used in making a final decision.Charges will apply per interpreter. Aldebaran Interpreting Services will indicate if this is necessary, and provide the name(s) of additional interpreter/s to the client.

Cancellation, No Shows

  • Cancellations on individual or intermittent requests, made less than two business days prior to the beginning of the assignment will be billed for the full amount. Cancellation policies on short term or long term contracts will be determined at the time of contract.
  • The full amount will be charged if the interpreter arrives to the assignment and either consumer or client does not. The interpreter will remain approximately 30 minutes before departing, after consultation with the organizer if possible. This will be applicable to all assignments with the full and agreed fee still payable.
  • No-shows are billed without exception.

Inclement Weather /Interpreter Absence Policy:

Great care has been taken to ensure preparedness to respond to most types of inclement weather. Service will be canceled, without cost, only when an emergency precludes safe driving conditions or a weather emergency has been declared by local officials and the interpreter cannot reach the assignment. Contact will be made regarding this at the earliest possible time.

No charges will be assessed if the interpreter must be absent from an assignment.

Please note, that NO SHOWS and LATE CANCELLATIONS are billed as scheduled.

*Policies are effective January 2014. Terms and rates apply at the time a formalized request form is submitted.
